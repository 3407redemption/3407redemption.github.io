Robot Progress: Redemption - Push Back (2025-2026)

Drivetrain Completed & Driving

Date: July 27

We finished our drivetrain that uses 6 wheels and is geared with a 36:48 gear ratio. Our drivetrain runs at an rpm of 450 and we believe it is a balanced approach that will allow for success in this game.

First Stage Intake Finished

Date: July 27, 2025

We built a flexwheel intake consisting of 5 flexwheels on a high strength axle spaced out slightly using high strength spacers. It is attached to two 10 hole L-Channels and braced over the top using a 3 wide C-Channel.
